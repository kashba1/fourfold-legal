The Challenge: Managing Leads and Cases

“Before Telecrm, Managing leads & Cases was chaotic & inefficient. — Manual process slowed us down; Telecrm helps us track the entire customer journey.”

Fourfold Legal is a specialized legal consultancy focused on helping individuals resolve loan repayment issues. From initial consultation to legal resolution, it demands clarity and zero miscommunication.

Where things started breaking

“We had leads coming in, calls going out — but no one could tell what happened next. Everything was in silos.”

Two Teams

To manage operations, Fourfold Legal had split its process between two teams—the Calling Team would speak to clients, qualify leads and The Advocate Team would handle legal resolution post-qualification.

Manual process

The entire flow was manual, fragmented and operated entirely on Excel. Leads were downloaded and distributed manually based on loan amount and type—a time-consuming process prone to errors and delays.

Bias on Lead sources

Callers developed bias based on lead sources—they would prioritize leads from certain campaigns and ignore others, creating an imbalance in follow-up quality and making it nearly impossible to fairly assess campaign performance.

No visibility

There was no dashboard to track whether calls were made, follow-ups happened, who paid, or where each lead stood in the funnel. Managers lacked an end-to-end view of every lead’s journey or team performance.

Chaotic & without context

After qualification, leads were passed to the legal team without context. Advocates had no idea what was discussed, what stage the case was in, or what communication had taken place. Documents were scattered, and handovers meant starting from scratch.

The result? Leads were falling through the cracks, follow‑ups were inconsistent, and the entire process was chaotic. Fourfold Legal needed a solution that could streamline operations, eliminate bias, and provide complete visibility into every lead’s journey.

The Solution

Fourfold Legal implemented Telecrm by creating two custom workspaces:

  • Calling Team Workspace to handle lead qualification and initial fee collection.
  • Legal Team Workspace to manage post‑qualification processes and legal consultations.

Here’s what changed:

Real‑time lead capture & Distribution

Lead sources were directly integrated with Telecrm—no manual downloads needed. Leads were automatically assigned to calling agents based on load amounts and case value, turning hours of Excel sorting into seconds.

Permission Templates to eliminate lead bias

Using permission templates in Telecrm, Fourfold ensured that lead sources are hidden from calling agents—only decision makers can view campaign‑level data. This removed bias and ensured every lead received equal attention and follow‑up.

Sales forms ensure smooth handover

Custom forms captured all client details upfront, making the transition from callers to advocates seamless—no more lost context or missing information.

Centralized Documentation management

All case documents and communication logs were stored in one place, giving managers and advocates complete visibility into each lead’s history.

Campaign Reporting and feedback loop

With Telecrm the marketing team could now see:

  • Which campaigns generated what kind of leads
  • How each campaign performed in terms of conversions
  • What happened post-lead — where it dropped and why

This feedback helped them refine targeting, budget allocation, and Ad creatives — something they could never do with their old system.

360 Manager visibility and performance insights

Managers have a compelete, top-down view of every lead flowing through the system- from the moment it’s captured to final resolution. They can see what happened at eacbh stage, track follow-ups, and monitor lead progress in real-time. Alongside this, they get detailed performance reports on botrh callers and advocates- making it easy to identify gaps, measure effectivemeness, and drive accountability across teams.

The Impact: From Manual Chaos to 2.5× Growth

Fourfold Legal has seen a dramatic transformation in both efficiency and revenue. Manual downloads, scattered documents, and lead confusion were replaced with real-time automation.
